Utopia is Back.

date

Boston’s biggest and best intercollegiate yacht party of the year.

Utopia tickets have SOLD OUT. If you purchased a ticket but can no longer attend, please request for a refund here. We are allowing refunds for a limited number of ticket holders.

If you are unable to purchase a ticket after regular ticket sales, there will be a very limited amount available for purchase at the door. Tickets will be first-come first-served, and are not guaranteed.

In order to reserve a spot, you must submit payment via Venmo (@harvardutopia2023) or Zelle ((617) 849-0451) within HALF AN HOUR of submitting this form.

If you successfully purchased a ticket, you should have received a confirmation email within 24 hours of your purchase (which may have gone to your Spam folder). If you did not receive a confirmation email, you were not approved for a ticket, even if you already submitted payment. If you already submitted payment but did not receive a confirmation email, please reach out to utopia@harvardcsa.org to receive a refund.

ATTIRE

Cocktail attire requested. It will be cold, so a coat is recommended. We will have a coat check onboard available for guest use.

ALCOHOL POLICY

Food or alcohol of any kind will not be permitted aboard the vessel. There will be a security check before boarding.

DEPARTURE TIME

The boat will depart at 8:00pm promptly. Final boarding will be at 7:45pm. If you are late, you will not be able to board the boat.
