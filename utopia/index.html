Utopia is Back.

Friday, October 10th @8PM

Boston’s biggest and best intercollegiate yacht party of the year.

Looking to party with CSA again? Join us on Friday, October 10th 8-10PM for Utopia, Boston's biggest annual intercollegiate yacht party! We are SO excited to bring back Utopia and can't wait to see you all there. Tickets are limited and tend to sell fast, so be sure to purchase yours right away when early bird ($25) sales begin at 10:00pm on Friday, October 3rd, or regular tickets ($30) at 10:00pm on Sunday, October 5th. Follow us on insta @harvardradcliffecsa and stay tuned for more information!

If you are unable to purchase a ticket after regular ticket sales, there will be a very limited amount available for purchase at the door. Tickets will be first-come first-served, and are not guaranteed.

In order to reserve a spot, you must submit payment via Venmo (@harvardutopia2025) or Zelle ((508) 808-3072) within HALF AN HOUR of submitting this form.

If you successfully purchased a ticket, you should have received a confirmation email within 24 hours of your purchase (which may have gone to your Spam folder). If you did not receive a confirmation email, you were not approved for a ticket, even if you already submitted payment. If you already submitted payment but did not receive a confirmation email, please reach out to utopia@harvardradcliffecsa.org to receive a refund.

ATTIRE

Cocktail attire requested. It will be cold, so a coat is recommended. We will have a coat check onboard available for guest use.

ALCOHOL POLICY

Food or alcohol of any kind will not be permitted aboard the vessel. There will be a security check before boarding.

DEPARTURE TIME

The boat will depart at 8:00pm promptly. Final boarding will be at 7:45pm. If you are late, you will not be able to board the boat.
