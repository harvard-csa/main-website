Frosh Week Overview

Join us as we kick off our fall 2023 events with our annual frosh week! We welcome freshman to learn more about CSA as an organization and get to know the board.

Schedule:

  • 9/11, 8:00 PM -9:00 PM: Info Session @ Emerson Hall 105
  • 9/13, 5:00 PM -7:00 PM: Open Dinner @ Mather Private Dining Room
  • 9/14, 7:00 PM -9:00 PM: Mooncake Study Break @ Science Center 8th floor Observatory
  • 9/15, 7:30 PM -9:00 PM: Fall Soiree @ Ticknor Lounge (in Boylston)
  • 9/16, 10:00 PM -2:00 AM: Asian Glow @ Lowell Underground

Follow our instagram @harvardradcliffecsa to stay updated with our frosh week events!
